Tesla Full Self-Driving (Supervised)

Comprehensive Evaluation Route Guide
2026 Model Y  |  Vancouver & Burnaby, British Columbia

This guide presents three purpose-built driving routes designed to exercise every major capability of Tesla Full Self-Driving (Supervised) across the Lower Mainland. Each route systematically exposes the system to residential streets, school zones, highway merging, complex urban intersections, bridge crossings, roundabouts, pedestrian-heavy areas, construction zones, and parking lots.

Tesla offers FSD demonstrations in the Vancouver area, and recent coast-to-coast Canadian FSD drives have started from this region, making it one of the most extensively exercised FSD territories in Canada.

Route 1 — Burnaby → Vancouver → Burnaby Recommended

Duration: 3–3.5 hours  |  Distance: ~95 km  |  Difficulty: High — covers every major driving scenario in one loop, including Deep Cove's winding village roads.

⚠ SAFETY REMINDER: Full Self-Driving is supervised. You must keep your hands ready to take control and remain attentive at all times. Be prepared to intervene if the vehicle encounters unexpected situations, especially in construction zones, around emergency vehicles, or in heavy pedestrian areas. Always obey all traffic laws regardless of FSD behaviour.
